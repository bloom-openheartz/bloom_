Community Guidelines & Crisis Resources

Bloom is a peer space, not a substitute for professional care. Please read this once — it keeps the space safe for everyone.

Community Guidelines

  • Be kind first. Assume good intent, and extend the grace you'd want back.
  • No shaming, harassment, hate speech, or unsolicited advice on someone's body or choices.
  • Protect anonymity — don't ask for or share identifying details of others.
  • No links, solicitation, or self-promotion in stories, chat, or the kindness wall.
  • This is a peer-support space, not therapy or emergency care. If you're in danger, use the crisis resources below.

Before you continue

A quick, honest rundown of how Bloom handles your data.

What's stored, and where. Your journal pages, mood log, saved affirmations, habit check-ins, and account info are stored only in this browser (localStorage). We don't run them through a server.

What's shared with the community. If you post to the Wall of Stories, the Kindness Wall, or a Chat Room, that content is visible to other visitors. Post anonymously any time you'd like using the toggle provided.

Your choices. You can skip anything, mute reminders with Do Not Disturb, and use the quick-exit link at any time. Clearing your browser data removes everything Bloom has stored for you.

Not a substitute for care. Bloom is peer support, not therapy or emergency services. See the Community Guidelines & Crisis Resources page for help beyond this app.
